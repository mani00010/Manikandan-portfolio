I am an NGS Scientist dedicated to advancing precision medicine through high-complexity genomic workflows. With deep technical expertise in HLA Typing, Whole Genome Sequencing (WGS), and Whole Exome Sequencing (WES), I specialize in designing and optimizing library preparation for both Illumina and MGI platforms.

My expertise spans the entire NGS lifecycle — from high-throughput DNA/RNA extraction to library construction, sequencing, and advanced troubleshooting. Currently serving as Scientist I at Strand Life Sciences, Bengaluru, working on clinical exome and WGS diagnostics.

I am passionate about bridging the gap between raw biological samples and actionable clinical insights to drive breakthroughs in medical research and genomic diagnostics.
